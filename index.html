Venue

HUB Seattle

220 2nd Avenue South, Seattle, WA 98104

(206) 430-6007

thehubseattle.com

HUB Seattle is located in a building that is, in a word, extraordinary. Housed in the historic Masin's furniture building in Pioneer Square, this unique facility will soon house one of the largest concentrations of social entrepreneurs in the country.

From the bike entrance that leads to basement storage and showers, to the on-site cafe with a grand staircase that leads from the 300 person events space on the ground floor to the desks, offices, and glass meeting rooms on the second floor.

Upper floors include classrooms with state of the art technology and lunch decks with brilliant views of downtown Seattle.

Hotel

Alexis Hotel

1007 1st Avenue, Seattle, WA 98104

(206) 624-4844

alexishotel.com

The Alexis Hotel is uniquely Seattle. Set in a series of Seattle City Landmark buildings, this downtown hotel is rooted in the city's wild Gold Rush days, mirroring its exciting, cutting-edge creativity.

Attached to the hotel, the Bookstore Bar is famous for its vast whiskey library, monthly scotch tastings and Chef David Hatfield's flavorful food pairings.

Yup, The Bookstore Bar really is a bookstore, too. The selection is as fun, eclectic and classy as our clientele. All books: $5. Looking: Free.
